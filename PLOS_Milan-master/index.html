What is PLoS?

The Pedestrian LOS Model is aimed at evaluating walking conditions on road and street corridors in urban environment. PLoS is a measure of comfort and safety of existing and planned walkways. It allows objective and sound evaluations of pedestriansâ€™ perception and response to roadway environment.






Implementation: Steps

Collect

Using EpiCollect our group collects measurament of data for PLoS and enhanced PLoS computation. In particular in eache street it was measured the value of:

  • Number of lanes;
  • On-street parking;
  • Buffer;
  • Width of sidewalk;
  • Curb ramps;
  • Lighting;
  • Widht of bike laneand shoulder;

    • While Traffic volume and the Vehicular speed are provided by Systematica. Then we store the results in a field survey and download them in .csv format.

    Processing

    We proced to:

  • Store the data collection using QGIS;
  • Make operations such as buffer, intersection and attribute table manipulation in order to link every point value to the corresponding road network segment;
  • Compute the PLoS and the enhanced PLoS





    • WebGIS develop

    Development of a website loaded on GitHub in order to show the result of the PLoS and enhanced PLoS computations

    Problems & Solutions

  • Accuracy: Points collected using Epicollect with smartphone, because of bad accuracy of the GPS, sometimes has wrong coordinates on the map. So we menage them changing their position on the map;
  • WFS Connection: during the webGIS development we have reported a problem adding a layer using a WFS Connection. It was suffcient to reproject the layer with the standard CRS for OpenLayers which is EPSG:3857


    • Possibility to query the points collected on the map
